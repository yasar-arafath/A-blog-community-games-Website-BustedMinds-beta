HEAL ME PLZ ★

Download Now

Made in 3 days for the 46th Ludum Dare, 5th best game overall out of 5000+ games, theme was "Keep it alive"


PLAY MORE FREE GAMES  :D 

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You’re playing the healer of one of the best team of adventurers ever,

getting ready to defeat one of the most dangerous dark lords in the world.

But, well, something goes wrong :  the evil wizard turns your teammates into children…

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Will you be able to reach and defeat the final boss ?
Controls : 
  • Left click to shoot healing orbs on your allies (be careful not to heal ennemies) 
  • Right click or hold to move to your mouse position.
  • Move to one of your allies to kick them to trigger their special attack
  • Q/W/E : Trigger your powers once you unlocked them
Pro tips :
  • Move to one of your allies to kick them to trigger their special attack
  • casting the E spell on an ally and then kicking them to trigger their special attack is  a great combo
